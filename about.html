Our Story

A softer idea,
born from a simple habit.

We noticed something: people don't actually use neck pillows. They rest on their arms. So we built a pillow for that.

Meet the Founder

Started by a student who was just tired of being uncomfortable.

Arm Pillow began the way most real products do — with a small, annoying problem. Long bus rides, late-night study sessions, red-eye flights. No pillow ever seemed to actually fit the way people rested in real life.

So our founder started sketching. Then sewing. Then testing. Arm Pillow is the result of asking real travelers and real students what comfort actually looks like on the go.

We're a young startup, but we're serious about one thing: making rest feel accessible no matter where the day takes you.

Our Mission
"To make better rest portable — so comfort is something you carry with you, not something you leave at home."
Our Journey

From sketch to soft reality.

Every prototype taught us something new. Here's how Arm Pillow took shape.

Step 01

The spark

Watching travelers and students fall asleep on their arms over and over. We realized the problem wasn't the person — it was the pillow.

Step 02

First prototype

A rough first attempt — just fabric, foam, and a lot of trial and error. Not pretty, but it proved the concept worked.

Step 03

User feedback round 1

We handed prototypes to real students and travelers and asked them to try it. Their feedback shaped everything that came next.

Step 04

Refined prototype

Softer materials, better fit, lighter feel. We kept iterating until it started to feel like something people actually wanted to keep.

Step 05

Validation

30 in-person interviews. 90% said they'd buy it. A clear price range emerged: $12–$15. That's when we knew we had something real.

Step 06

What's next

We're preparing for a first manufacturing run, building the brand, and getting ready to put Arm Pillow in the hands of travelers everywhere.
