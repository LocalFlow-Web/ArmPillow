The Product

Meet the Arm Pillow.

A portable, adjustable pillow that wraps around your arm — so comfort travels with you.

The idea

Rest that fits the way you already sit.

Arm Pillow is a soft, lightweight pillow that adjusts to your arm. It's the simplest way to turn any seat — a plane, a train, a library chair — into somewhere you can actually relax.

No straps. No setup. Just wrap, lean in, and breathe.

How it works

Three steps. That's it.

1

Wrap

Slide Arm Pillow comfortably around your forearm.

2

Adjust

Fit it to the way you naturally rest — no straps, no fuss.

3

Relax

Lean in and let comfort do the rest. Anywhere, anytime.

Why it's different

Not another awkward travel gadget.

We didn't want to build another clunky accessory you'd regret packing. Arm Pillow is intentionally small, soft, and minimal — the kind of thing you actually want in your bag.

It solves a real problem without creating new ones.

Who it's for

Designed for travelers
and students.

Whether you're catching a flight across the country or catching a nap between classes, Arm Pillow is built to travel with you. It lives in your bag, not in the way of it.

  • Flights, trains, bus rides
  • Libraries, dorms, lecture halls
  • Commutes, road trips, waiting rooms
  • Any seat that wasn't built for naps
